Frequently Asked Questions about Team Training

Q: How many people can attend?

A: Each course is one full day, with up to 70 attendees. Please contact us if you have larger class sizes.

Q: Can we do multiple topics or courses?

A: Yes, you can select several course topics and schedule multiple days of training.

Q: Is Team Training eligible for UX Certification credit?

A: Yes. Participants in the workshop will be eligible to earn 1 UX Certification credit for each team training course they attend. In order to earn a UX Certificate, each attendee must attend 5 different courses, and pass an online exam for each course. Courses can be completed in a single week,or spread out over time, and attended at a Team Training, at a UX Conference, or a combination.

Q: What’s the difference between “Team” and “Intensive” training?

A: Our team trainings focus on mastering the most important concepts and UX techniques, using example materials we provide. By contrast, in the Intensive Applied format, you will work with your real problems, your real customer data, and your real constraints to begin identifying actionable solution.
