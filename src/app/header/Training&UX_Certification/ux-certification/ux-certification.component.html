Add a Specialty to Your UX Certification

Take 5 courses in 1 of our 3 Specialty areas (interaction design, research, or management) to get the Specialty added to your UX Certification. You can still get UX Certified by taking 5 courses across the 3 different Specialties.

Iteraction Design

Learn best practice principles that you can apply to any design situation, including web, mobile, complex and simple apps, and consumer products.

Reserch

Learn how to conduct UX research methods correctly to gather sound data to improve the user experience. Get hands-on practice.

Management

Learn to apply known UX and management practices to effectively plan, communicate, negotiate, and measure UX work, people, and process.
